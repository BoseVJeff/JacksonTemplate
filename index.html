Department of Biological Sciences

About Department

Department of Biological Sciences at IISER-Bhopal started functioning in August 2008 with the enrolment of the first batch of students for the BS-MS integrated course. The aim of the department is to foster knowledge in students pertaining to basic and advanced areas of biological sciences by not only pursuing both teaching and high-quality research but also through integrating biology with physics, chemistry and mathematics. The academic and research contributions of the faculties in this department have been recognized in the form of publications, projects, fellowships and awards.

BS-MS Dual Degree Programme

We offer a five year dual degree programme for students who have passed class XII. The first year of the programme consists of core courses in basic sciences, the 2nd, 3rd, and 4th years are dedicated to specialization in chosen minor and major subjects. The final and fifth year includes a research project from your chosen major subject.

Graduate Studies

Graduate Studies

Students admitted to the PhD program are officially associated with a faculty member at the time of registration. The first year comprises of rigorous course work wherein students can register for any course offered within IISER-Bhopal or through the National Knowledge Network (NKN) from other Indian institutions. The students may start their research project in their first semester or later based on their PhD advisers recommendation. The course work allows students to develop a comprehensive understanding of the concepts aiding them in their research. Subsequently, students are also evaluated for their suitability to pursue their PhD through several systems, including a comprehensive examination and a graduate seminar. In addition to being involved in research with a particular mentor, students are also motivated towards presenting their research work as departmental seminars and to publish their research findings in peer-reviewed international scientific journals and conferences. After successful completion of the research work, the thesis dissertation submitted by the student is evaluated by a panel of reviewers and is then recommended for the award of a PhD degree.
